Version 1 For research Closed

What we are testing

This prototype will be used to test an end-to-end transfer check scenario. This scenario will require the user to make use of CCaRD, CRDS, R&S and Adjustments.

In this round, we aim to understand:

  • How do users feel about being able to review all historical court case info and calculate dates all in one system?
  • What order do they go through our system when conducting a transfer check?
  • Do users ever want to go back and review info? (eg before moving to the next product)?
  • Do users feel this will meet the outcomes (generated in the design away day)?
  • What other touch points / services / offline processes do they use for our journey?
  • Do users do the whole process of a transfer check themselves? or handover to someone else for any part of it?
  • Understand if the court cases screen meets the needs of users in a checking mindset?
  • Validate our service architecture with users - ie having separate tabs for court cases, adjustments etc
